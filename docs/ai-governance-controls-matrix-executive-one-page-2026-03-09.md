# Executive One-Page: AI Governance Controls for Production Delivery

## Why this matters
AI value only scales when trust, safety, and operational control scale with it.

This one-page framework shows how governance controls become a delivery accelerator: faster approvals, fewer incidents, and higher production confidence.

---

## Control Pillars (Executive View)

1. **Ownership & Accountability**
- Clear RACI for every AI workflow
- Defined decision authority and escalation path

2. **Risk-by-Lifecycle**
- Controls from design to monitoring
- Business-context risk scoring (not generic checklists)

3. **Secure AI Engineering**
- Security-by-design, privacy-by-design
- Threat modeling + CI/CD security gates

4. **Data, Privacy, and Traceability**
- Data minimization and access controls
- Full change lineage for model/prompt/config/data updates

5. **Safety & Reliability Controls**
- Prompt-injection and abuse defenses
- Output guardrails + human-in-loop for high-risk cases

6. **Monitoring & Incident Response**
- Real-time drift/performance/security alerts
- Rollback/kill-switch and remediation SLAs

7. **Third-Party AI Risk Management**
- Vendor due diligence and dependency visibility
- Ongoing compliance and operational assurance

8. **Standards & Regulatory Alignment**
- Align to NIST/ISO/privacy obligations
- Operate beyond minimum compliance

## Portfolio Mapping (Proof of Capability)

### A) Production RAG Support Assistant
- Safety guardrails, citation enforcement, eval harness, drift monitoring

### B) AI Workflow Automation (Lead-to-Action)
- Human override, SLA controls, routing governance, auditability
- Demonstrates measurable ops improvement with controlled automation

### C) Mission Control Governance Layer
- Baseline trends, regression gates, history tracking
- Demonstrates change-aware governance at delivery velocity

## Executive KPIs to Track

- Regression events prevented by gates
