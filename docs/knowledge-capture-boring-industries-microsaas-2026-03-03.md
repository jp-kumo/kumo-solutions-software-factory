# Knowledge Capture — “6 Boring Industries Begging For Micro-SaaS”

Captured: 2026-03-03

## Core thesis
The best near-term Micro-SaaS opportunities are in “boring” sectors with high operational pain and low software fit from broad tools. Money follows compliance risk reduction and admin-time elimination.

## Industries highlighted
1. Manufacturing compliance systems
2. Construction/field-service safety workflows
3. Transportation/logistics document normalization
4. Small-business back-office digitization
5. Agriculture (ERP-lite for niche operations)
6. Niche compliance/safety workflows

## Repeating product pattern
- Pick one painful workflow that is still paper/spreadsheet driven.
- Build trade-specific logic the generic tools don’t support.
- Sell outcomes: fine avoidance, hours saved, reduced legal risk.
- Keep UX simple and offline-capable where field conditions require.

## GTM pattern
- Validate pain directly with local business calls.
- Ask for value estimate and paid beta before building full product.
- Use niche trust channels (brokers, paralegals, yards, local operators).

## Kumo fit (recommended focus)
Most aligned opportunities for Kumo now:

### A) HVAC/field compliance sidecar
- Core promise: avoid compliance misses and reduce closeout friction.
- MVP: required-field hard stops, photo evidence, one-click audit export.

### B) Contractor lead + operations automation
- Core promise: faster response and fewer missed opportunities.
- MVP: inbound intent triage, routing, reminders, and KPI digest.

### C) Trade-specific intake/document workflow
- Core promise: reduce manual document handling time.
- MVP: strict form validation + formatted output + CRM handoff.

## 7-day validation sprint
1. Pick 1 vertical and 1 workflow.
2. Conduct 5 pain-validation calls.
3. Secure 1 paid pilot commitment.
4. Implement narrow MVP with one measurable KPI.
5. Deliver before/after report and convert to monthly retainer.

## Pricing posture
Avoid commodity pricing. Use tiered outcome-based offers:
- Readiness/Blueprint
- MVP Automation
- Production System + monitoring/governance
