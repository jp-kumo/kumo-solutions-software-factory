## Quick take
This is a viable business model **if executed with clear positioning, policy-safe content, and reliable production cadence**.

## What’s usable for us
- Montage format from short clips (good for throughput)
- Script-first production pipeline (batchable)
- Multi-channel monetization logic (TikTok/YT + digital products)
- Simple toolchain that can be operationalized into SOPs

## Main risks
1. Platform policy risk (synthetic persona disclosure, misleading “real person” presentation)
2. Quality drift risk (voice/style inconsistency across clips)
3. Monetization volatility risk (RPM swings, algorithm dependence)
4. Reputation risk if “undetectable/deceptive AI” framing is used

## Recommended business version (Kumo-safe)
Build a **“Synthetic Spokesperson Content System”** offer for SMBs and creators:
- Outcome: consistent short-form educational content + lead magnet funnel
- Positioning: transparent AI-assisted media production (no deception framing)
- Deliverables:
  - Persona/voice guide
  - Weekly script pack
  - 3–5 shorts per week
  - Basic performance dashboard (retention, CTR, watch time)
  - Optional Etsy/lead-magnet product tie-in

## 30-day pilot plan
- Week 1: niche + content pillars + persona + policy-safe disclosure language
- Week 2: produce first 9 short clips + 3 long montage cuts
- Week 3: publish cadence and A/B titles/hooks
- Week 4: optimize based on retention + CTA conversion

## Go / No-Go criteria
- GO if: consistent weekly output + early retention signs + no policy flags
- NO-GO if: repeated moderation issues or high edit overhead breaks margins

## Notes for Taste & Power
- Use this as a **production acceleration pattern**, not identity deception.
- Prioritize documentary authority + repeatable format over “AI influencer” novelty.
