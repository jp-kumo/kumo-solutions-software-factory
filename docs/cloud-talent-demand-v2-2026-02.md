## 1) Executive Summary (what matters for you)

1. U.S. demand remains strongest where cloud intersects with **security, reliability, automation, and AI/data platforms**.
2. The safest “high-employability” core stack for 2026 remains: **AWS/Azure/GCP + Terraform + Kubernetes + CI/CD + IAM/security + observability**.
3. **Cloud security** and **SRE/platform** continue to command compensation premiums versus generic cloud engineering.
4. **FinOps is moving from “nice-to-have” to hiring differentiator** as organizations focus on spend governance and AI-cost controls.
5. For your pivot, the best positioning is: **secure, cost-aware cloud/AI delivery for regulated environments** (directly aligned to your life-sciences history).
6. Most directional hiring domains from March 2026 onward: **Financial Services, Healthcare/Life Sciences, SaaS/AI platforms, Public Sector, Industrial/Logistics**.
