## 1) Objective
Increase qualified lead conversion by reducing response delays and enforcing a consistent follow-up system.

## 2) Current friction (business impact)
Most local service teams lose opportunities when first response is delayed, follow-up is inconsistent, and handoff is unclear.

Likely impact:
- Lower contact rates
- Fewer booked appointments
- Revenue leakage from missed/late responses

## 3) Scope & Deliverables
Kumo will implement a practical lead-response system focused on speed, consistency, and visibility.

### Deliverables
1. **Lead intake workflow map**
   - Sources documented (website/forms/email/inbox)
   - Lead routing and ownership logic defined

2. **Immediate response automation**
   - Initial response message framework
   - Time-window and escalation rules

3. **Follow-up sequence (Day 0/1/3/7)**
   - Message templates by lead stage
   - Re-engagement branch for non-responders

4. **Response + conversion KPI dashboard**
   - First-response time
   - Contact rate
   - Appointment-set rate
   - Follow-up completion rate

5. **Handoff SOP + admin guide**
   - Step-by-step operating procedure
   - Failure/recovery checklist
   - 30-day optimization recommendations

## 4) Timeline (30 days)

### Week 1 — Discovery + design
- Process interview
- Current workflow audit
- KPI baseline capture
- Automation blueprint approval

### Week 2 — Build + configure
- Implement response and follow-up flows
- Configure tagging/status progression
- Prepare templates and escalation rules

### Week 3 — Testing + launch
- End-to-end scenario testing
- Team handoff/training
- Go-live and monitored launch

### Week 4 — Optimization
- Tune timing/copy based on actual performance
- Deliver KPI report + next-step plan

## 5) Success metrics
- Median first-response time reduced to target SLA
- Increase in contact rate
- Increase in appointment-set rate
- Improvement in follow-up completion consistency

## 6) Investment
- **Setup (one-time): $1,250**
- **Monthly support/optimization (optional): $350–$600/mo**

## 7) Assumptions
- Client provides access to existing communication/workflow stack
- Client assigns one decision-maker and one day-to-day point person
- Approvals are provided within agreed windows to maintain timeline
