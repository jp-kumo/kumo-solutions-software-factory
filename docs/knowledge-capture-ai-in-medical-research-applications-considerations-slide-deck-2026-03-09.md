**Presenter:** Dr. Jim Angle  
**Group:** Health Information Management Working Group (Cloud Security Alliance context)
**Release date (from deck):** September 26, 2024

## Key points captured
- AI/ML/DL is reshaping medical research and healthcare workflows.
- Drug discovery applications include:
  - de novo drug design
  - retrosynthesis and reaction prediction
  - protein engineering
- Cognitive Digital Twin concepts are highlighted for personalized medicine and proactive risk identification.
- Cross-cutting concerns include ethical/legal risk: privacy, bias, transparency, and governance obligations.
- Collaboration and open data/model development are emphasized as acceleration levers.

## Implementation relevance for regulated AI portfolio work
- Aligns with production-grade AI requirements: traceability, evaluation, bias controls, privacy safeguards, and legal readiness.
- Supports portfolio framing around secure, auditable AI in healthcare-like/high-risk environments.
