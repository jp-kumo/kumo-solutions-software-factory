Kumo Solutions will implement a workflow that converts meeting transcripts/notes into structured action items with owners, due dates, and priority.

---

## 2) Objectives
1. Reduce post-meeting admin time.
2. Improve follow-through on action items.
3. Provide consistent, readable task summaries after meetings.

---

## 3) In-Scope Workflow
- Input: meeting transcript or notes
- Processing:
  - extract decisions, actions, owners, due dates
  - generate task list with priority labels
- Output:
  - task summary in agreed channel (email/Slack/Telegram/Notion/Trello)
  - weekly digest of open actions

---

## 4) Deliverables
1. Configured pilot workflow for one team/process
2. Up to 8 processed meetings during pilot
3. Action-item outputs per meeting
4. End-of-pilot baseline vs. outcome snapshot
