# Target Agent List — Little Rock / North Little Rock / Conway

Use this as your prospecting sheet. Focus on agents/teams with lead volume and clear commission upside.

## ICP (Ideal Client Profile)
- 8+ active listings OR visible paid lead activity
- Solo top producer or small team (1–8 agents)
- Uses CRM/lead forms but has inconsistent speed-to-lead
- High-value transactions where one saved deal matters

## Prioritized segments
1. **Top-producing solo agents** (fastest close path)
2. **Small broker teams** (need routing + follow-up discipline)
3. **Investor-focused agents** (care about speed + qualification)
4. **Luxury-focused agents** (high commission, high ROI story)

## Prospect tracker fields
- Name
- Brokerage
- Market (LR / NLR / Conway)
- Niche (buyer / seller / investor / luxury)
- Lead sources (FB, Zillow, website, referrals)
- CRM (if known)
- Est. monthly lead volume (L/M/H)
- Contact channel (email/IG/phone)
- Last contact date
- Next follow-up date
- Stage (Lead/Qualified/Discovery/Proposal/Won/Lost)
- Notes

## 20-target sprint plan
- Build list of 20 targets:
  - 8 Little Rock
  - 6 North Little Rock
  - 6 Conway
- Send 10 personalized messages day 1
- Send 10 personalized messages day 2
- Follow-up sequence: +48h, +4d, +7d

## Message personalization hooks
- Mention neighborhood/zip they post about most
- Mention listing activity cadence
- Mention after-hours challenge (showings/weekends)
- Tie to one metric: "contact leads within 5 minutes"

## Qualification checklist before proposal
- Budget approved?
- Decision-maker on call?
- Problem urgency acknowledged?
- Lead sources + CRM confirmed?
- Pilot start date agreed?
