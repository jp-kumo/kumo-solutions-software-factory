# CRM Relationship Tracking

Use this skill to keep relationship follow-through reliable.

## Do this
- Track: contact, context, opportunity type, stage, last touch, next action, due date.
- Enforce no-contact staleness checks (e.g., >14 days without follow-up).
- Keep notes short and decision-relevant.
- Always log the next action after each interaction.
