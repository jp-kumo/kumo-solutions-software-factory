# Income Streams Strategy

Use this skill to design Jacques's path to 7 income streams.

## Framework
- Score opportunities by: speed-to-first-dollar, effort, risk, scalability, fit.
- Build in phases:
  1) Stabilize cash flow (freelance/consulting)
  2) Add semi-scalable offers
  3) Add long-term asset streams

## Do this
- Maintain a ranked backlog of streams.
- Define entry experiment, KPI, and stop/continue criteria for each stream.
- Review monthly and rebalance effort.
