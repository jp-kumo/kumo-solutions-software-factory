# Market Signal Scanner

Use this skill to capture actionable market signals.

## Do this
- Monitor job and freelance demand patterns for cloud/AI services.
- Extract recurring requirements, tooling expectations, and pain points.
- Turn findings into concrete actions: project tweaks, offer updates, messaging changes.

## Cadence
- Weekly: quick scan + 3 actionable recommendations.
- Monthly: trend summary + positioning adjustment.
